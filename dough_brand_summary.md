# Dough Brand Summary & Guidelines Memorization

This document provides a highly detailed summary of the brand assets, messaging, and design system extracted from the **Dough Company Profile 2026** and **Dough Brand Guidelines Final**.

---

## 1. Brand Identity & Overview

### 1.1 Brand Story
* **Core Philosophy:** In a world overflowing with noise, Dough chooses to "knead the silence." They start with the belief that great ideas are like dough—soft, raw, and full of potential. With the right hands, the right heat, and time, they rise.
* **Identity:** Dough is a creative agency / creative consultancy. They treat every brand like a blank canvas, every brief like a fresh batch, and every challenge like a recipe waiting to be reimagined. They blend strategy with art, insight with instinct, and local flavor with global vision.
* **Focus Areas:** Branding, content creation, events, influencer campaigns, and general creative work. "Ideas don't just sit. They rise."
* **Establishment Date:** est. 2024

### 1.2 Mission & Vision
* **Mission:** To take raw ideas and shape them into something real and moving. Dough listens closely, thinks boldly, and creates work that builds deep human connections rather than just basic content.
* **Vision:** To create a world where brands connect, laugh, dance, and leave something meaningful behind. They aim to build a creative space that feels like a warm kitchen, shaping brands slowly, intentionally, and with heart. They do not chase trends; they create from the inside out.

### 1.3 Brand Slogan
> **"where ideas take shapes"** (or **"where ideas take shapes."**)

### 1.4 Brand Values
1. **Creativity:** Molding ideas into unique realities.
2. **Innovation:** Pushing boundaries and sparking fresh solutions.
3. **Holistic:** Seasoning every aspect of a brand with strategy and heart.
4. **Flexibility:** Versatile and adaptable to any challenge.

### 1.5 Brand Voice
* **Energetic, youthful, and inspiring!**
* Aiming to spark creativity and innovation with warmth, wit, and playfulness ("because who says business can't be a little playful?").

### 1.6 Brand Persona
* **Name:** Dough
* **Age:** 24
* **Job Title:** Creative Designer
* **Attributes:** Strategist, versatile, energetic, youthful, inspiring, creative, flexible, adaptable, trend-aware, go-getter.
* **Services Provided:** Marketing, branding, content creation, media strategy, production, F&B (food & beverage) consulting, and more.

## 3. Typography System

The typographic language is designed to feel minimal, clean, and clear. Montserrat is used for all Latin text, Alexandria for all Arabic text, and Galak Round specifically for the logo wordmark.

### 3.1 Font Choices
* **Logo Wordmark Font:** **Galak Round** (used in small caps to maintain softness and minimalism with soft, clean edges).
* **Latin Font:** **Montserrat** (bold, geometric, highly legible, wide letter width). Used in all brand applications.
* **Arabic Font:** **Alexandria** (matches the Latin font perfectly and is used across different weights).

### 3.2 Latin Font Settings (Montserrat)
* **Headline:** Montserrat Extrabold | Size: **70pt** | Leading: **70pt**
* **Sub-headline (Bold):** Montserrat Bold | Size: **30pt** | Leading: **33pt**
* **Sub-headline (Medium):** Montserrat Medium | Size: **25pt** | Leading: **28pt**
* **Body Text (Regular):** Montserrat Regular | Size: **12pt** | Leading: **14pt**
* **Captions / Small Text:** Montserrat Regular | Size: **9pt** | Leading: **10.5pt**

## 4. Logo Guidelines

### 4.1 Logo Composition
* The wordmark features lowercase-style typography spelling `dough` with a registration/copyright mark `©`.
* The letter **"o"** is replaced with a soft, organic, light-blue dough blob shape.

### 4.2 Clear Space
* The minimum clear space on all sides of the logo must be equal to the height/width of the letter **"u"** in the logo wordmark.

### 4.3 Minimum Sizes
* **Print Applications:** No less than **30mm x 7.7mm**
* **Web/Digital Applications:** No less than **85px x 22px**

### 4.4 Color Combinations & Single Color Rules
* The logo should only be presented in approved color combinations that guarantee high contrast.
* **One-Color Variations:**
  * Solid Dark Navy version on light backgrounds.
  * Solid White version on dark backgrounds.
* Approved combinations include:
  * Dark navy text with light-blue "o" on Light background.
  * White text with light-blue "o" on Dark navy background.
  * Dark navy text with medium-blue "o" on Sparkle blue background.
  * White text with light-blue "o" on Water blue background.

### 4.5 Logo Misuse (Prohibited Actions)
* Do **NOT** stretch or squeeze the logo.
* Do **NOT** change the wordmark font or use uppercase letters (e.g., `DOUGH` or `D UGH` are incorrect).
* Do **NOT** outline the logo.
* Do **NOT** add drop shadows.
* Do **NOT** rotate or tilt the logo.

### 4.6 Logo Placement
* Recommended alignments:
  * **Square layout:** Positioned in any of the four corners (left or right).
  * **Rectangle layout:** Center-aligned or aligned in any of the four corners (left or right), keeping comfortable breathing space.

## 5. Graphical Elements & Patterns

### 5.1 The Dough Shape
* The main graphical asset is the **dough icon** (the blob).
* It is designed to be changeable and dynamic, showcasing the agency's flexibility. 
* Five main variations are established, all maintaining soft, circular/oval-like edges without sharp corners.

### 5.2 Character & Iconography
* The dough shapes can be adapted into playful characters with cartoon-like white arms and legs.
* These characters are playful, quirky, and interactive, adding humanized charm to brand communications.
